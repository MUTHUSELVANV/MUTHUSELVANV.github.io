Jan 2025 – Apr 2025

Project Consultant
Children’s Emergency Services (CES) Improvement Initiative

Michigan Medicine, Ann Arbor, MI, USA

Conducted process mapping, stakeholder interviews, and time studies to improve pediatric admission efficiency in the Emergency Department. Developed a predictive ML model using XGBoost to estimate wait times, supporting dynamic staffing decisions. Built a discrete-event simulation in ProModel to evaluate throughput under resource constraints and discharge lounge implementation. Proposed high-impact solutions including discharge lounge usage, admission standardization, and predictive dashboards, resulting in an estimated 11% reduction in average patient wait time and improved care flow efficiency.

Tools: Python, Tableau, ProModel, Simrunner, Microsoft Excel

May 2025 – Aug 2025

Research Assistant
Reducing Emergency Department Crowd

Center for Healthcare Engineering & Patient Safety, Ann Arbor, MI, USA

Analyzed CT imaging delays impacting patient disposition and length of stay by conducting time studies, shadowing staff, and developing a simulation model. Identified lack of a standardized patient-calling protocol, leading to 10–20 minute scanner idle times. Incorporated contrast preparation times into the model to assess effects on queue length and system flow. Findings showed that reducing scanner downtime could significantly improve throughput and reduce overall LOS.

Tools: Python, Tableau, Microsoft Excel
